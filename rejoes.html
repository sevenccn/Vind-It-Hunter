Rejoes: Second hand

Rejoes, the cool new vintage spot in town, has been buzzing with good vibes since it opened. According to locals, it's the go-to place for awesome deals and a friendly atmosphere. People love that the prices are fair and won't break the bank. The staff at Rejoes seem like a friendly bunch, although the community admits they don't know much about them; besides the fact that they're nice at the checkout. Our own visit confirmed the positive vibes and we started to do some digging. The store had a bit of everything, from fancy stuff to leather gear, jeans, and cool shirts. We were pleasantly surprised, just like everyone in the neighborhood. In the end we even got ourselves an item for a decent surprise.
