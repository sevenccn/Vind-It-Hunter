About us!

Hey, we are team "Vind'it Hunter" We're a bunch of vintage addicts who live for the thrill of digging through thrift stores. Why? 'Cause we're all about snagging awesome outfits without burning a hole in our wallets.

It's not just about the clothes; it's a whole lifestyle. We thrive on the excitement of finding hidden gems, hearing the stories behind each piece, and, of course, getting killer deals.

Our team is dedicated to seeking out stores that not only provide quality clothing at affordable prices but also align with our values. We are keen explorers, investigating whether these establishments are as environmentally friendly as they claim. It's important to us that the places we support are committed to reducing waste and promoting eco-conscious practices/sustainability.

We actively seek stores that resonate with our love for the planet and provide affordable fashion. Yet, it's more than just about the clothes it's the entire experience. We connect with the awesome store folks, soak in the unique atmosphere, and revel in the thrill of finding those unbeatable bargains.
